About me

I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). My supervisor is Prof. Qiang Yang. Before joining HKUST, I obtained the B.Eng degree from School of Data and Computer Science, Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU) [Resume].

Feel free to contact me via email.

Research interests

My current research is Low-Resource Natural Language Processing (LR-NLP). Revolving around this goal, I mainly study the following topics:

  • Machine Learning: transfer Learning, meta-learning, multi-task learning, graph embedding.
  • Natural Language Processing: sentiment analysis, opinion mining, information extraction, question answering.
